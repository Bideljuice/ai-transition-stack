# Content Funnel Optimizer

## When to Use This Skill
Every piece of content Tam creates targets one specific
funnel level. This skill ensures the angle, language,
and CTA match exactly where the reader is — not where
Tam wants them to be.

Never write for all levels at once.
One post. One level. One job.

## CRITICAL
- Always identify funnel level BEFORE writing anything
- Never mix levels in one post — pick one and commit
- Language must match the level — technical language 
  for Level 4, simple emotional language for Level 1
- CTA must match the level — awareness for Level 1,
  action for Level 4
- Level 3 is the core audience — default here when 
  level is unclear

## Steps

Step 1: Identify the content input
Receive idea, highlight, or rough topic from Tam.

Step 2: Detect funnel level
Check against references/funnel-levels.md
Ask: who is this content actually for?
- Completely unaware? → Level 1
- Tried it, dismissed it? → Level 2
- Knows it matters, overwhelmed? → Level 3
- Ready to act, needs blueprint? → Level 4

Step 3: Present level decision
Show Tam:
"Funnel level: [Level X — name]
Target reader: [one sentence describing them]
Angle: [what will reach them at this level]
CTA style: [awareness / reframe / simplify / convert]
Confirm? Y to continue."

WAIT FOR USER: Y or redirect

Step 4: Optimize for level
Adjust the content brief or draft:
- Level 1: lead with human stakes, not AI features
- Level 2: extend time horizon, don't argue today
- Level 3: radical simplification, one next step
- Level 4: concrete, specific, tactical

Step 5: Pass to writing
Hand off to my-content-capture or my-content-router
with funnel level context included.

## Important
- Level 3 gets 2-3 posts per week — most content here
- Level 1 gets 1 post per week — broad reach play
- Level 2 gets 1 post per week — reframe the dismissive
- Level 4 gets 1 post per week — convert the ready ones
- Never write Level 4 content to a Level 1 audience —
  it will feel alienating and they'll tune out

## Note
- Tam was Level 3 six months ago — 
  write Level 3 content from the inside, not outside
- "Willing but not sure where to start" = 
  the five words that define the core audience
- Reference funnel-levels.md for exact language,
  tone, and example angles per level
