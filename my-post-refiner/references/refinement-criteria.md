# Post Refinement Criteria

## Pass 1 — AI Writing Check
Remove every tell before anything else.

- [ ] No unnecessary headers or sections
- [ ] No random bolding or italics
- [ ] No bullet points inside bullet points
- [ ] No "Honestly" or "Actually" mid-sentence
- [ ] No tricolons: "not just X, it's also Y, and even Z"
- [ ] No "on one hand / on the other hand" structure
- [ ] No corporate "just noticed" framing
- [ ] No self-referential language
- [ ] No over-explanation — point made once, move on

## Pass 2 — Inspiration Check
Does it move the reader?

- [ ] Contains at least one line that removes an excuse
      OR reframes something familiar unexpectedly
- [ ] Reader feels something — not just learns something
- [ ] Has one line that could standalone as a single tweet
- [ ] Takes a clear position — no sitting on the fence

## Pass 3 — Voice Check
Does it sound like Tam specifically?

- [ ] Casual — like a smart friend, not a consultant
- [ ] No jargon — simpler word always wins
- [ ] Short sentences — max 2 lines each
- [ ] No paragraph longer than 3 sentences
- [ ] Morgan Housel feel — story-driven, one idea per paragraph
- [ ] Could NOT have been written by anyone else

## Pass 4 — Density Check
Every word earns its place.

- [ ] No filler sentences that restate what was just said
- [ ] No setup paragraphs — gets to the point immediately
- [ ] No trailing sentences after the landing
- [ ] Each sentence pulls reader to the next

## Pass 5 — CTA Check
Does it end with intention?

- [ ] CTA is direct action OR genuine reflection question
- [ ] Not generic ("follow for more" is rejected)
- [ ] Direct action when reader can do something TODAY
- [ ] Reflection question when post is about mindset/identity
- [ ] "Ugly is fine" energy — removes the excuse to not act

## Passes 6-10 — Line by Line
Go through every single line.
For each line ask: "Does this earn its place?"
If no — cut it or rewrite it.
After each pass, check all 4 criteria above again.

## Stopping Rule
If post passes all checks before pass 10 — deliver immediately.
If post still fails after pass 10 — flag to Tam:
"This draft has a structural problem. 
Recommend starting fresh with a new angle."
