# Content Brief Quality Checklist

## Before delivering the brief, verify:

### Topic & Angle
- [ ] Topic is specific — not "AI" but "why most people 
      misunderstand what AI will actually replace"
- [ ] Angle is contrarian or unexpected — not the obvious take
- [ ] Core insight is ONE sentence — sharp, not vague

### Target Reader
- [ ] Brief speaks to someone navigating job/financial 
      security in the AI revolution
- [ ] Assumes reader is smart but not technical

### Format-Specific Checks

LinkedIn Post
- [ ] Can be read in 60 seconds
- [ ] Hook is in the first line — not a setup, a statement
- [ ] One idea only — no tangents

Long-form Article
- [ ] Has a clear argument — not just a topic
- [ ] 3 supporting points identified
- [ ] Research or data points noted if needed

Email Newsletter
- [ ] Subject line angle is specific and curiosity-driven
- [ ] Has one main story or insight — not a roundup
- [ ] Personal tone — written to one person, not a crowd

### CTA
- [ ] CTA is direct action OR genuine reflection question
- [ ] Not generic ("follow for more" is rejected)
- [ ] Matches the post type — action for habits/tools, 
      reflection for mindset/identity
