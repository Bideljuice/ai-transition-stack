# AI Transition Stack
### A Claude skill system for people navigating the AI era

Built by Tam Lam — pharmacy manager turned AI builder.
This is the content system I use to create consistent, 
sharp content about the AI transition.
Built in public. Shared openly.

---

## The Problem This Solves

Every day there's a new headline.
AI is taking jobs. AI is changing everything.
AI will replace you. Or maybe it won't.
Nobody seems to agree.

So you do what most people do.
You close the tab. You feel vaguely uneasy.
You move on.

My content exists for people stuck in that loop —
overwhelmed by the noise, not sure where to start,
willing to figure it out but needing a clear first step.

That's who I write for.
This is the system that makes the writing consistent.

---

## Who The Content Is For

**The core audience: willing but not sure where to start.**

People who:
- Have a stable job but sense something shifting
- Feel overwhelmed by AI news — not inspired by it
- Have tried ChatGPT, shrugged, and moved on
- Want someone to cut through the noise and say:
  here's what's actually happening, here's where to start

Not developers. Not tech founders.
Normal people with normal jobs who feel the window closing.

---

## The Content Funnel

Every piece of content targets one specific awareness level:

| Level | Who they are | Content goal |
|---|---|---|
| 1 — Unaware | "AI is overblown" | Create awareness through human stakes |
| 2 — Dismissive | "It's just a tool" | Extend time horizon, don't argue today |
| 3 — Overwhelmed | "I can't keep up" | Radical simplification, one next step |
| 4 — Ready | "Tell me where to start" | Concrete blueprint, convert to follower |

**Core audience is Level 3.**
Most content targets here — 2-3 posts per week.

## The Skills

| Skill | What it does |
|---|---|
| `my-content-funnel` | Identifies which audience level to target |
| `my-content-router` | Decides format and angle automatically |
| `my-content-capture` | Turns anything you read into a post |
| `my-content-brief` | Plans content before writing |
| `my-post-refiner` | Pressure-tests drafts before publishing |
| `my-writing-style` | Writes in your voice — not generic AI voice |

---

## How It Works

You read something interesting. You paste it.
The system identifies who it's for, picks the format,
writes the draft, tightens it — in your voice.

You read it. You approve or reject.

No prompt engineering. No blank page.
No "I don't know what to write today."

Just paste. Approve. Publish.

---

## Installation

1. Download or clone this repo
2. Upload each skill folder to Claude.ai:
   Settings → Capabilities → Skills → + → upload zip
3. Enable all six skills
4. Open a new chat and paste anything interesting
   you've read today
